FAQ

Common Questions

Do you charge for consultations?
No, our initial consultations are completely free of charge. We are typically paid by the lenders, meaning you get expert advice at no cost to you.
What is an LMI waiver?
Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI) is usually required if you have less than a 20% deposit. However, many allied health professionals qualify for waivers, allowing you to buy with as little as 5-10% deposit without the extra cost.
Can you help if I'm a locum or contractor?
Absolutely. We specialize in irregular income patterns, including locum work, casual shift loading, and private contracting.
Which professional bodies do you work with?
We work with AHPRA registered professionals including Nurses, Psychologists, Physiotherapists, Chiropractors, and more.
